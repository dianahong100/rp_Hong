The Impact of Genres of Music on Brain Activity When Memorizing a Series of Patterns

Background

  • Different genres of music have varying effects on the mind
  • When listening to music, the brain analyzes the components of the music
  • Different genres with distinct components will have different effects on the brain
  • This study investigates how genres of music affect brain activity during cognitive functioning

Rationale

  • Places with lower education efficiency tend to be worse off than those with higher ones
  • Utilization of music to harness the complete capacity of human learning still in progress
  • This study contributes more data and information to further drive this movement toward educational growth
  • Study sought to find which parts of music best enhances brain function to the greatest extent

Conclusion

  • Hypothesis (shoegaze most stimulates beneficial cognitive activity) supported by the data
